Andrew Ross Sorkin

Author of Too Big to Fail

November

New York Times columnist and author, Andrew Ross Sorkin, has been described as the most famous financial journalist of his generation.

His book, Too Big to Fail, was the first behind-the-scenes account of the financial crisis that led to our current recession. The Economist, The Financial Times, and Business Week all named it one of the best books of the year.

The book was published by Viking on October 20, 2009. It was later adapted into a movie by HBO Films and premiered on May 23, 2011.

The cast of the movie: Too Big to Fail

  • William Hurt as Hank Paulson
  • Paul Giamatti as Ben Bernanke
  • Billy Crudup as Timothy Geithner
